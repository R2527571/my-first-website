HEALTH SERVICE TO DIABETES diabetes

diabetes mellitus

signs and symptoms

  • passing urine a lot especially at night
  • sores and abscesses that take time to heal
  • blurred vision
  • excessive hunger

management of diabetes
  1. consult the nearest doctor of health servic
  2. maintain health body weight by doing exercises
  3. reducing the intake of sugar,salt and oily foods
  4. avoiding excessive alcohol intake and stop smoking

basically diabetes is a chronic medical condition characterised by glucose in the blood.It is subdivided into type 1 , type 2 and gestational diabetes.Type 1 the body attacks and destroy insulin producing cells in the pacrease.Type 2 is caused due to resistant or insufficient insulin production.gestational develops during pregnacy usually resolving after birth.
